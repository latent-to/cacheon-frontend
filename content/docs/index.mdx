**Cacheon** is Bittensor Subnet 14 (SN14). It is an open, on-chain competition for **production-grade inference optimization** of LLMs.

Miners submit a **containerized inference server** that serves a fixed model via an OpenAI-compatible API. Validators run every submission against a vLLM baseline on the same hardware and score it on **time-to-first-token** (**TTFT**), **throughput**, and **output correctness**. The best server takes the throne; all token emission flows to the king until someone beats them.

## Why Cacheon exists

Inference has been the bottleneck for serving LLMs at scale. Latency, memory, and cost determine whether a model is usable in production. There is no shortage of optimization techniques—_FlashAttention_, _PagedAttention_, _speculative decoding_, _KV cache quantization_, _custom kernels_—but they are benchmarked in isolation and results are impossible to compare fairly.

Cacheon turns the problem into a live, reproducible contest: same model, same hardware, same prompts, same scoring. Miners are free to use any technique, any language, any runtime. The only thing that matters is serving the model faster while keeping outputs correct.

The product-market fit is straightforward: if Cacheon can consistently produce the fastest and most cost-efficient way to serve a high-value open-source model, then developers will route inference through us because better performance directly improves their product margins and UX. Over time, that creates a clear business model: Cacheon becomes both the optimization layer where better inference systems are discovered and the serving layer that monetizes demand for production-grade inference. It can serve agentic workflows, developer APIs, and other inference-heavy products that depend on fast, reliable long-context generation.

See [Why it matters](/docs/concepts/why-it-matters) for the full problem statement.

## What the subnet is not

- **Not a training subnet.** Weights are fixed at `Qwen2.5-72B-Instruct`. You do not finetune.
- **Not a generic hosting platform.** You serve one specific model. The competition is about serving it faster than everyone else.
- **Not a free trial service.** Miners are limited to one commitment per hotkey. Test locally before you register.
